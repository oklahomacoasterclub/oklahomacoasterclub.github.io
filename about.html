welcome to the club
Here's how it all started

The Oklahoma Coaster Club was created by Club President, Paul Davis, in March of 2015. His passion for roller coasters and amusement parks came from the pure joy that he got every time he visited a park. His home park, Frontier City, worked with him to do host the club's first title event called Ride For A Cure Day which raised money for the Muscular Dystrophy Association. The purpose behind the event was to raise money to find a cure for Muscular Dystrophy, which is a muscle-debilitating disease to which no cure has been found.

The club's motto is to 'Ride For a Cure'. This slogan was created in the hope of raising awareness to the fact that some people can't enjoy all of the activites that so many of us cherish, such as riding rides at an amusement park. Paul's brother, Travis Davis, had Muscular Dystrophy and lived for 20 years before losing his life to the disease. Our goal is to raise awareness for people like Travis.
